Frequently Asked

Shipping & Order FAQs

Which courier do you use for delivery? ⌄
We ship all orders exclusively via DTDC Courier & Cargo across India. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a DTDC tracking number to monitor your shipment.
Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available? ⌄
No, we do not offer Cash on Delivery. All orders are prepaid only. We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and popular Wallets. This helps us process and ship your order faster.
When do I get free shipping? ⌄
Shipping is FREE on all prepaid orders of ₹499 and above. For orders below ₹499, a flat shipping charge will be applied at checkout.
How long does delivery take? ⌄
Orders are typically delivered within 4–7 business days depending on your location. Remote areas may take a couple of extra days. You'll get a tracking link once your order ships.
What is your return policy? ⌄
We offer a 15-day return window from the date of delivery. Products must be returned unused and in their original sealed packaging. Please contact us on 98226 41098 to initiate a return.
How do I contact customer care? ⌄
You can reach us at 📞 98226 41098 — Monday to Saturday, 10am to 7pm. We're happy to help with orders, returns, product queries, and bulk orders.
